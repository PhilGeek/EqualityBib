EqualityBib by PhilGeek

Bibliography of philosophical papers on equality

A bibliography of relatively recent philosophical papers on equality compiled principally by Véronique Munoz-Dardé. The bibliography will be maintained separately from [Philosophy.bib](http://philgeek.github.com/bib/), though it will be merged into Philosophy.bib.

Dependencies

[BibTeX](http://www.bibtex.org/)
